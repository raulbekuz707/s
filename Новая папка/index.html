Looking for hobby and leisure magazine subscription deals?

Look no further than magazine.co.uk. Whether you're interested in arts and crafts, photography, health and fitness, history, or travel, we have a wide variety of hobby magazine subscriptions which will suit your needs.

Arts and crafts magazines: Whether you're a knitter, love to sew, sell your crafts, or you're an artist, magazine.co.uk have a wide range of arts and crafts magazines for your needs. With top arts and crafts titles like The Artist, Cardmaking and Papercraft, Cross Stitch Crazy, and Knit Now, you'll be spoilt for choice!

History magazines: Have an interest in history and the past or know someone who does? A subscription to one of the top history magazines in the UK would go down a treat, whether it's a treat for yourself or a gift for someone else. Choose from a variety of titles, including BBC History, History of War, and History Revealed.
Games, Music, TV and Technology magazines: Entertain yourself with one of the many entertainment magazine subscriptions offered by magazine.co.uk, including Total Film, Gamesmaster, Empire, Vintage Rock, The Stage and TV Times.

Country Life magazines: Love the countryside? Whether you live there or aspire to, then why not learn more about the British countryside and the countryside life with a great value subscription with magazine.co.uk. Choose from titles such as BBC Countryfile, Discover Britain, Countryside, Shooting Times, Country Life, and The Field.

Health and Fitness magazines: If you're passionate about looking after your body and overall health, there's a great selection of health and fitness magazine subscriptions to choose from at magazine.co.uk. Titles such as Women's Health, Runner's World, Men's Fitness, Triathlon, What Doctors Don't Tell You, Athletics, Your Fitness, Running Fitness and Muscle & Fitness.

Home, Cooking and Gardening magazines: If you're looking for great value home, cooking and gardening magazines, then look no further than magazine.co.uk. We offer subscriptions to top magazines, including titles such as Real Homes, Good Housekeeping, Ideal Home, Living Etc, and House Beautiful.

Photography magazines: Whether you're a professional or amateur photographer, here at magazine.co.uk we offer subscriptions to some of the best photography magazines in the UK. Brush up your skills or learn about the new and best photography equipment by subscribing to titles such as Digital SLR Photography, Advanced Photographer, Practical Photography, Photo Professional and Digital Camera magazines.

Travel Magazines: If you love to travel, then why not subscribe to a great travel magazine such as World of Cruising, Conde Nast Traveller, National Geographic Traveller, Lonely Planet, Coast, or Living France?

Other Hobby and Interest magazines: There's a wide range of other hobby magazine subscriptions to choose from at magazine.co.uk - from equestrian sports with Horse magazine, fishing with Angler's Mail, country sports like Horse and Hound and Gun Mart, boating magazines like Yachting Monthly and Practical Boat owner, and car magazines such as Mercedes Enthusiast magazine.
